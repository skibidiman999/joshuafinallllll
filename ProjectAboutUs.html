EL1TE Microchips

About Us

EL1TE Microchips is an Singaporean corporation and technology company headquarted in Singapore. It is a company which designs and supplies semiconductors, graphics processing units (GPUs), and application programming interfaces (APIs) for data science and high-performance computing.

Founding

EL1TE Microchips was founded on January 23, 2000, by Teo En Ming (CEO as of 2024), an American electrical engineer; Kurt Tay, an engineer who worked at Watermelon Microsystems; and Sylee, who was previously a senior staff engineer and graphics chip designer at Watermelon Microsystems. The three men agreed to start the company in a meeting at a roadside Mcdonalds in Jurong, Singapore.

The three co-founders were frustrated at their previous company's management and were looking to leave, they discussed a vision of the future which was so compelling that they decided to leave their previous companies and co-founded EL1TE Microchips

The three co-founders envisioned that the ideal trajectory for the forthcoming wave of computing world would be in the realm of accelerated computing, specifically in the semiconductor industry.
